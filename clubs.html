Clubs

Coding Club

Join us to learn programming, participate in hackathons, and build amazing projects. We focus on competitive programming, web development, and open source contributions.

Robotics Club

Explore robotics, automation, and embedded systems. Build robots, participate in competitions, and work on innovative hardware projects with cutting-edge technology.

Design Club

Express your creativity through UI/UX design, graphic design, and digital art. Learn design principles, tools like Figma and Adobe Suite, and work on real projects.

Literary Club

For book lovers, writers, and poets. Organize reading sessions, writing workshops, poetry slams, and publish our annual literary magazine featuring student works.

Music Club

Celebrate music in all its forms. From classical to contemporary, instrumental to vocal, we organize concerts, jam sessions, and music production workshops.

Photography Club

Capture moments and tell stories through the lens. Learn photography techniques, photo editing, and participate in photo walks and exhibitions across campus.

Drama Club

Bring stories to life on stage. Act, direct, write plays, and perform in our annual theater productions. A perfect platform for aspiring actors and theater enthusiasts.

Quiz Club

Test your knowledge and learn new things. Participate in quizzes on various topics, from science and technology to pop culture and general knowledge.
